**Solution**: Diversify across geographies.

## Risks of NASDAQ 100

### 1. Heavy Tech Concentration

**Risk**: If tech sector crashes, NASDAQ suffers significantly

**Historical Examples**:
- **Dot-com Bubble (2000-2002)**: NASDAQ fell 78%
- **2022 Tech Correction**: NASDAQ fell 33%

**Mitigation**: 
- Don't go 100% NASDAQ
- Balance with Indian equity
- Long-term holding (recovers from crashes)

### 2. Top-Heavy Concentration

**Risk**: Top 10 companies = 50% of index

**What If**: Regulatory action against big tech?

**Example**: Antitrust cases against Google, Meta, Amazon

**Mitigation**: This is index investing‚Äîautomatic rebalancing happens

### 3. Currency Risk (Double-Edged Sword)

**Usual Case**: INR depreciates ‚Üí You gain  
**Rare Case**: INR appreciates ‚Üí You lose

**Example**: ‚Çπ65 ‚Üí ‚Çπ60 (INR strengthens)
- NASDAQ +10% in USD
- Currency -7.7%
- **Net**: +2.3% in INR

**Historical Reality**: INR has depreciated consistently for decades. Unlikely to reverse long-term.

### 4. Regulatory and Geopolitical Risks

**Scenarios**:
- US-China trade war affecting tech companies
- Increased regulation on big tech
- Data privacy laws impacting business models

**Mitigation**: Diversification across 100 companies, long-term view

## Tax Considerations (TCS)

### TCS (Tax Collected at Source)

**Rule**: If LRS remittance > ‚Çπ10 lakhs/year ‚Üí 20% TCS

**Example**:
- Remit ‚Çπ12 lakhs for investment
- TCS: ‚Çπ2.4 lakhs (20% of ‚Çπ12L)
- **You pay**: ‚Çπ14.4 lakhs upfront

**But**:
- TCS is **not a tax**, it's **advance tax**
- Fully adjustable when filing ITR
- Can be adjusted via **Form 12BAA** against salary TDS

### Form 12BAA (Employer TCS Adjustment)

**Old Process**:
- Pay TCS upfront ‚Üí Wait for ITR filing ‚Üí Get refund (6-12 months)

**New Process (Form 12BAA)**:
- Submit Form 12BAA to employer
- Employer adjusts TCS against monthly TDS
- No waiting for refund!

**How**:
1. Download Form 12BAA from Income Tax portal
2. Fill details of TCS paid
3. Submit to employer HR/Finance
4. Employer reduces TDS from salary
5. Get TCS money back in monthly salary

**Example**:
- TCS paid: ‚Çπ2.4L (Oct 2024)
- Monthly TDS: ‚Çπ20,000
- Submit Form 12BAA
- Employer stops TDS for 12 months
- You get ‚Çπ20,000 extra in salary each month

## Tax Filing (ITR-2)

### Additional Schedules Required

**Schedule FA** (Foreign Assets):
- Disclose IBKR account
- Disclose ETF holdings
- Value as of 31st Dec

**Schedule FSI** (Foreign Source Income):
- Dividends from US ETF (if any)
- Capital gains on sale

**Why Mandatory?**:
- Black Money Act 2015
- Non-disclosure ‚Üí Heavy penalties
- Better to comply and sleep peacefully

### Capital Gains Taxation

**STCG** (Holding < 24 months):
- Taxed at slab rate

**LTCG** (Holding ‚â• 24 months):
- Taxed at 12.5% without indexation

**Example**:
- Buy: ‚Çπ10L worth XNAS

- Gain: ‚Çπ8L
