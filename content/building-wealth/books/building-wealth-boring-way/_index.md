## Key Principles

This book is built on time-tested principles:

1. **Start Early**: Time is your biggest advantage
2. **Invest Consistently**: Automate to remove emotion
3. **Diversify Globally**: Don't put all eggs in one country
4. **Keep Costs Low**: Index funds over active funds
5. **Stay Disciplined**: Ignore market noise
6. **Think Long-Term**: 20-30 year horizon
7. **Rebalance Annually**: Maintain target allocation
8. **Protect First**: Insurance and emergency funds before aggressive investing
