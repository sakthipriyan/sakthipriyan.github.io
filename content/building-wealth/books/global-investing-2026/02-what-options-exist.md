## TODO: What Options Exist?

- **Domestic Mutual Funds** with global mandate (FoF, feeder funds)
  - Pros: Simple, rupee-denominated, no LRS needed
  - Cons: Limited universe, expense ratios, SEBI overseas investment cap
- **Indian ETFs** investing globally (e.g., Motilal Oswal Nasdaq 100 ETF, Mirae Asset NYSE FANG+)
  - Pros: Exchange-traded, lower TER
  - Cons: Tracking error, liquidity issues, same SEBI cap constraint
- **Direct International Investing via LRS**
  - Pros: Full universe of global ETFs, cost-efficient at scale, no SEBI cap
  - Cons: Requires LRS remittance, FX costs, brokerage account setup, tax complexity
