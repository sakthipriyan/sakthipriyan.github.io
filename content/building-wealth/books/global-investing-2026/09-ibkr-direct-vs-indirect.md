## TODO: IBKR Direct vs IBKR via ICICI Direct or Paasa

- Three ways to access IBKR from India:
  1. **IBKR Direct** — open account directly with Interactive Brokers
  2. **IBKR via ICICI Direct** — through ICICI Securities' international investing platform
  3. **IBKR via Paasa** — through Paasa's partnership with IBKR

- Comparison across all three:
  | Dimension | IBKR Direct | IBKR via ICICI Direct | IBKR via Paasa |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | Account opening | IBKR global | ICICI Direct | Paasa |
  | KYC | IBKR KYC | ICICI KYC | Paasa KYC |
  | FX conversion | IBKR IdealFX | ICICI bank rate | Paasa/partner rate |
  | LRS handling | Self | ICICI handles LRS | Paasa handles LRS |
  | Mobile app | IBKR mobile (full) | ICICI international tab | Paasa app |
  | Desktop (TWS) | Available | Not available | Not available |
  | Instruments | Full IBKR universe | Subset | Subset |
  | Fees | IBKR fees | ICICI fees + IBKR | Paasa fees + IBKR |
  | Irish ETF access | ✅ | To verify | To verify |

- **Mobile/WebApp access**: Indirect accounts (via ICICI Direct or Paasa) do not provide access to IBKR's desktop platform (Trader Workstation). Only mobile and web access is available.
