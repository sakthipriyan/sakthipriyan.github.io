### Key Insight

The wrong question: *"Will markets give me 12%?"*

The right question: *"Am I modeling reality?"*

Three lies most SIP calculators tell you:
- ❌ "Ignore inflation" — looks easier than it is
- ❌ "Ignore exit tax" — looks simpler than it is
- ❌ "SIP stays flat" — looks harder than it is
