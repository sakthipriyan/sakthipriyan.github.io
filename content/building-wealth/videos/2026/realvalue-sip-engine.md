## About This Video

This video provides a comprehensive demonstration of the **RealValue SIP Engine**, a sophisticated investment planning tool designed specifically for Indian investors.

### What You'll Learn

- How to use the three target modes: Time, Money, and Time+Money
- Understanding the difference between Nominal and Real (inflation-adjusted) values
- Setting up investment parameters: current investment, monthly SIP, growth rate, and hikes
- Interpreting post-tax real returns for accurate financial planning
- Using the URL sharing feature to save and share investment plans
- Analyzing monthly and yearly investment projections with interactive charts

### Key Features Covered

1. **Target Modes**
   - Target by Time: Calculate portfolio value for a fixed investment period
   - Target by Money: Calculate time needed to reach a target amount
   - Target by Both: Calculate required monthly SIP to reach target in fixed time

2. **Real Value Analysis**
   - Inflation-adjusted returns for accurate planning
   - Post-tax calculations for realistic projections
   - Present value of all contributions

3. **Comprehensive Visualizations**
   - Investment and Growth charts (yearly/monthly granularity)
   - Monthly contribution plans with automatic step-ups
   - Interactive ECharts for data exploration

4. **URL Sharing**
   - Save your investment plan in a compact URL
   - Share scenarios with family or financial advisors
   - All inputs preserved in the URL hash

### Why RealValue?

Most SIP calculators show inflated future numbers without accounting for inflation. RealValue focuses on **what your money is actually worth** — showing post-inflation, post-tax outcomes that help you make realistic financial decisions.
