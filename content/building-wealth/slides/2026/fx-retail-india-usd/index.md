### 2️⃣ Introducing FX Retail 🌐

<div class="r-stack">

<div class="fragment fade-in-then-out">
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;"><strong>FX Retail</strong> is a forex dealing platform launched in <strong>August 2019</strong>.</p>
<p style="font-size: 1em; margin-top: 15px; color: #6b7280;">
  Built by the <strong>Clearing Corporation of India (CCIL)</strong><br/>
  under a directive from the <strong>Reserve Bank of India (RBI)</strong>.
</p>
</div>

<div class="fragment fade-in-then-out">
<p style="font-size: 1.1em;">Created to give <strong>retail customers</strong> — individuals and small businesses — direct access to the <strong>interbank forex market</strong>.</p>
<p style="font-size: 0.95em; color: #6b7280; margin-top: 15px;">Before FX Retail, you had no choice but to accept whatever rate the bank quoted.</p>
</div>

<div class="fragment">
<p style="font-size: 1.2em; color: #10b981;"><strong>With FX Retail, you:</strong></p>
<ol style="font-size: 1em; margin-top: 15px; text-align: left; display: inline-block;">
<li class="fragment fade-up">Lock a forex deal at a <strong>negotiated rate</strong></li>
<li class="fragment fade-up">Get a <strong>Trade ID</strong></li>
<li class="fragment fade-up">Complete remittance via your bank using that Trade ID</li>
</ol>
<p class="fragment" style="font-size: 1em; color: #f59e0b; margin-top: 15px;">→ Bank settles at <strong>your locked rate</strong>, not their default.</p>
</div>

</div>
