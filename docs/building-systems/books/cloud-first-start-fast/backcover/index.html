About the Author

Sakthi Priyan H is a seasoned software engineer and technical leader based in Chennai, with over 16 years of industry experience and a lifelong passion for building systems that solve real-world problems. His journey in programming began at the age of 11, driven by a deep curiosity and a love for technology that continues to shape his work today.

Currently serving as a Senior Technical Manager at Aqfer India Pvt Ltd, Sakthi leads engineering efforts on large-scale data platforms and distributed systems. His previous roles at companies like Crayon Data and Infosys have spanned domains from digital marketing to logistics, earning him recognition for both his technical depth and leadership.

Sakthi holds a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Coimbatore Institute of Technology, where his academic and extracurricular achievements—such as winning Bosch’s Best Project award—reflected both innovation and initiative.

Outside of work, Sakthi is an independent developer, having built a variety of tools and bots across web and mobile platforms. He also enjoys blogging, reading, photography, and exploring topics in economics and history.
