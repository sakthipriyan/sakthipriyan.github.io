₹1 Crore Home Loan — The Reality (Nominal vs Real Cost)

What a ₹1 Crore home loan at 8% truly costs in nominal vs real terms across 10, 15, and 20 year tenures.

📅 Mar 5, 2026 ⏱️ 07:54 📺 YouTube

About This Video

This video breaks down the real cost of a ₹1 Crore home loan at 8% and explains why the scary nominal interest number does not tell the full story. You will compare 10, 15, and 20 year tenures and see how inflation changes the burden over time.

What You’ll Learn

  • Why a 20 year loan showing ~₹1.01 Cr interest can be misleading in isolation
  • Difference between nominal EMI and inflation-adjusted (real) EMI
  • Side-by-side comparison of 10, 15, and 20 year loan outcomes
  • Why tenure should be chosen based on cashflow, not fear of nominal totals
  • How lower EMI can create investing optionality

Scenarios Covered

Key Insight

The wrong question: “How much total nominal interest will I pay?”

The right question: “What EMI fits my cashflow, and can I invest the difference productively?”
