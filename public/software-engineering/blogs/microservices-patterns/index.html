Microservices Architecture Patterns

Microservices have revolutionized how we build and deploy applications. In this post, we’ll explore the most important patterns that every architect should know.

Core Patterns

1. Service Registry

A service registry acts as a database of available services, their instances, and their locations.

2. Circuit Breaker

Prevent cascading failures by implementing circuit breakers between services.

3. API Gateway

Provide a single entry point for all client requests to your microservices.

Implementation Strategies

When implementing microservices, consider:

  • Data consistency across services
  • Inter-service communication patterns
  • Monitoring and observability
  • Security at the service level

Conclusion

Microservices patterns provide proven solutions to common distributed system challenges. Choose patterns that fit your specific use case and organizational context.
